WELCOME TO MINESWEEPER!

Starting up the game:
1. Open the Minesweeper.py file on your computer.
2. Press the F5 key on your keyboard.
3. Enjoy!

How to Play:
1. Left click a square to open and reveal its state.

Rules:
1. If you click on a square containing a mine, you lose.
2. If you open all the non-mine containing squares, you win.
3. Have fun!
